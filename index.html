Data Privacy Notice & Consent

We protect the privacy and security of the personal information you share with us. This notice describes what we collect on our registration form, why we collect it, and how we safeguard it.

Summary

We collect only what is needed for registration and event management, keep it for a limited time, and do not use it for recurring marketing unless you explicitly opt in separately.

What we collect

When you register, we collect the following personal and professional details:

  • Full Name
  • Medical Specialty
  • Hospital / Institution Affiliation
  • Signature

Purpose of collection

The primary reason for collecting this information is for the Contractual Necessity of managing your registration. Your data allows us to:

  • Maintain accurate records of attendees.
  • Verify your identity and professional credentials.
  • Log attendance for professional credit (where applicable).
  • Follow up with a single communication regarding event materials or post-event feedback.

Legitimate interests

We have a legitimate interest in using your professional information to:

  • Improve future InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc. events.
  • Aggregate anonymized data to understand the professional backgrounds of our audience.

Use of information & limitations

We will NOT:

  • Use your registration information to send you recurring marketing materials or unrelated sales pitches unless you explicitly opt-in separately.
  • Contact you for unrelated purposes once the event and its short-term follow-up are concluded.
  • Sell your personal information to any third parties.

Share & disclosure

We do not share your information outside InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc., except:

  • With your consent, if you chose to share your details with a specific sponsor.
  • Where required by law, such as to regulatory or licensing bodies.

Data retention

We will keep your personal data for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this notice. For event records, this is typically for a period of one year, after which it will be securely destroyed or anonymized.

Your rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, request corrections, object to certain processing, and the right to have your data erased.

Consent

By filling out the registration form, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Data Privacy Notice. You consent to the collection and processing of your personal information by InnoGen Pharmaceuticals Inc. for the primary purposes of registration and event management.
